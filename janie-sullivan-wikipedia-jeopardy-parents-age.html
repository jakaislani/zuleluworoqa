What Is Janie Sullivan Job? Her Career Details

Janie has been serving at Vitality Group Inc. in Greater Chicago Area for 7 years and 4 months now. Currently, she holds the position of Scrum Master of Go-to-Market in the organization since October 2022.

Janie works as a Scrum Master at Vitality Group Inc. (Source: Janie’s LinkedIn)

Janie initially joined the company in March 2016 as its Wellness Strategy Manager. She held the position till July 2022 and then took the role of Operations and Market Specialist of Client Relations.

Prior to joining Vitality Group, Janie served as an Account Manager at ComPsych. She worked there for 1 year and 7 months from September 2014 to March 2016.

As per her LinkedIn bio, Janie was a Senior International Client Account Manager at Celergo LLC from October 2010 to September 2014. Previously, she served as an English Teaching Assistant at Academie de Poitiers for 9 months from September 2009 to May 2010.

Janie started her professional career working as an Administrative Assistant at Default Servicing Technologies, LLC in 2009. She also held the same position at Summit Valuations for 2 years and 8 months.
