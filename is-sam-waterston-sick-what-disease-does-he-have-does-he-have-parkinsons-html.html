Does Sam Waterston Have Parkinson’s?

Parkinson’s Disease is a neurological condition affecting the brain’s Substantia Nigra, causing damage to nerve cells and reducing dopamine production. Symptoms include tremors, slurred speech, slowed movement, and coordination difficulties. While there’s speculation about Sam having Parkinson’s, there’s currently no confirmation, making it reasonable to assume he doesn’t have any underlying conditions or undisclosed illnesses.
