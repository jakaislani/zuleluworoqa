He is accused of s*xually assaulting a 10-year-old girl several times while performing vision tests. It was his first alleged s*xual assault that occurred in January 2018 in Garden City at the Douglas Elementary School.

His second was accused of s*xually assaulting a 13-year-old girl, and he allegedly blindfolded her and s*xually assaulted her. This incident happened in March 2018 at Marshall Upper Elementary School in Westland.

According to the press release, the third was accused of s*xually assaulting an 11-year-old girl at Douglas Elementary in May 2019. Like the second one, he also allegedly blindfolded her and s*xually assaulted her.

The fourth girl James Baird s*xually assaulted is a 15-year-old girl and also blindfolded by him. This incident happened at Stevenson Middle School in February 2020.

The second time he was arraigned in the 21st District Court in Garden City Wednesday, he was given a $15,000 personal recognizance bond. At 9 in the morning, there will be a probable cause conference on 13th Feb, and a preliminary examination is scheduled for 10 a.m. on 27th Feb.
