Carlota Ciganda is a professional golf player who hails from Spain. She has excelled on both the LPGA & European Tours throughout her career. She has competed for Team Europe in three Solheim Cups. 

Ciganda rose to fame when she had a career-high nine top 10 finishes in 2018. Not only that, she competed for Spain in the Rio de Janeiro Olympics in 2016. She was voted Player of the Year & Rookie of the Year in her first season, 2012, and went on to win the LET’s Order of Merit. 

She became a professional in May 2011, and the following month she made her LET debut at the Tenerife Ladies Match Play, where she came in second behind Becky Brewerton. During that season, she participated in the Ladies European Tour Access Series and won the Murcia Ladies Open.
