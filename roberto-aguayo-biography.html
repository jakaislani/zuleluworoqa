Who is Roberto Aguayo?

Roberto Aguayo is an American football player. He currently plays as a kicker for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League (NFL).

He played college football at Florida State University. While he was at the University, he was the most accurate kicker in Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC) history and third in NCAA history.

Aguayo was born on May 17, 1994, in Mascotte, Florida, USA. He is the son of American parents Martina Aguayo and Roberto Aguayo Sr.
