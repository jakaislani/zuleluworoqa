Mariyam Khalif is a Pakistani child actress who is working in the Pakistani TV industry and has acted in a variety of serials. She is mainly known for the role in “Parchaiyaan”, “Bashar Momin”. She was born on 11th April 2007, hailing from Karachi, Pakistan where she studied and started her career. She got huge acclaim for versatility and her overloaded cuteness. While her fans adore her for her amazing acting skills that won the hearts of many.

Career

At the age of 7, Mariyam Khalif got the chance to work in many popular TV serials and incredibly, she has established herself at such a young age. She has acted in a large number of serials and also appeared in various commercials. Besides, she has worked for many notable brands and products. She took the start of her career with a debut serial “Parchaiyaan” in which she appeared as Mariyam. While it was aired on ARY Digital in 2012. In 2013, Mariyam Khalif worked in the serial “Meri Beti” in which she appeared as Iraj. While the serial was aired on ARY Digital. In the same year, she appeared in “Koi Nahin Apna” as Shiza and it was aired on ARY Digital. One of her best serials includes “Bashar Momin” in which she portrayed the role of Pareezay and it was aired on Geo TV. In 2015, she worked in the serial “Karb” in which she appeared as Emaan and Dua in “Dil Ka Kia Rang Karun” both were the best projects of HUM TV in 2015. In 2016, she worked as Fatima in HUM TV’s serial “Sanam” and as Zubi in HUM TV’s “Gila”. In 2017, she was last seen in the serial “Gustakh Ishq” in which she appeared as Dua and it was aired on Urdu1. Alongside this, she also worked in TV commercials including Q-Mobile G- 200, Sunsip Thanda Orange, and others are notable.

Achievements

The youngest actress Mariyam Khalif has achieved huge applause and much recognition in her career life and still struggling to gain more success.
